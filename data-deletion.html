Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2025

In accordance with Facebook/Instagram Platform policies, we provide users with a method to request deletion of their data collected through our app.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you have used our app and wish to have your data deleted, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to erikcelliott@gmail.com with the subject line: “Instagram Data Deletion Request”.
  2. In the body of the email, include your full name and the Instagram username associated with the app.
  3. We will process your request and delete all associated data within 7 business days.

We do not permanently store any personal data on our servers. All access tokens or profile information used are temporary and solely used for providing the intended functionality of the app.
